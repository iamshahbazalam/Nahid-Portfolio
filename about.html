About Me – Nahid Akhtar

I am Nahid Akhtar, a dedicated and performance-driven Civil Engineer with over 5 years of hands-on experience in executing large-scale infrastructure, power, and urban development projects across India, Saudi Arabia, and the Maldives. With a strong foundation in construction management, structural execution, and quality assurance, I bring a well-rounded approach to civil engineering—blending technical precision with practical site experience.

Currently engaged with L&T Saudi Arabia LLC on the prestigious 380kV Substation & Transmission Line at NEOM (NGHC Project), I am deeply involved in project planning, material estimation, site supervision, and coordination with clients and multidisciplinary teams. My background also includes key roles in landmark initiatives such as Lodha The World Towers (Mumbai), Greenfield Highway under Bharatmala Pariyojana (Rajasthan), and interior renovation works at Kanuhuraa Water Villas (Maldives).

  • Project Planning & Billing: Skilled in BOQs, RA bills, budgets, and detailed progress reporting.

  • Cross-functional Coordination: Effective liaison between clients, sub-contractors, and teams for seamless execution.
